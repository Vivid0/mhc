Wine Club

When you sign up for the Morgan Hill Cellars wine club, you go three for three. That means you purchase three wines every three months and you get a 20% discount to the standard price.

You can choose any wine you wish from our extensive wine list.

Pick them up at the tasting room or have them shipped to you.

Seasonal Recommendations

Not sure which wines to buy? Don’t worry! We’ve got you covered.

Each quarter, we’ll send out seasonal recommendations to help you chose wines that are typical for the particular time of year.

Our current summertime recommendations are:

White Zinfandel, Chardonnay and Riesling

The Club that keeps on giving

Morgan Hill Cellars Wine Club members enjoy the 20% discount not only on the three seasonal purchases but on any wines they purchase at any time.

Even better, for single purchases of over 12 bottles, members are granted a 30% discount!

Keep your wine cellar stocked without breaking your bank account.
