La Fenice Tasting Room

The La Fenice Tasting Room & Gift Gallery is an elegant place for you to relax, unwind and enjoy the Italian-Mediterranean architecture featuring gold and terra cotta walls, tiled floors, and redwood wine bars for tasting our fine wines.

The Gift Gallery specializes in tapestries, hand painted glassware and wine glasses, Italian pottery, clocks, wrought-iron wine racks, handmade candles and other wine related items.

Learn the story of how this tasting room got its name and the history of Morgan Hill Cellars.

At Morgan Hill Cellars, tasting is always free and the service is always friendly.

Choose 6 wines from our list of over 30 varietals! Allow our staff to guide you through the list with tailored recommendations or blaze your own trail and make your own selections. Best of all, there is absolutely no cost or obligation.

Pick the one you like best and purchase a full glass, a bottle or a case!

Relax on our outdoor patio in the beautiful, Northern California weather.

The patio is open to the public. Bring a picnic lunch to go with your favorite wine and relish the atmosphere while breathing the fresh air beneath a lovely pergola adorned with tangles of grape vines.
